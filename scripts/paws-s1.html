Pulse Reels — Original Series Script

Paws & Billionaires

Season 1  |  12 Episodes  |  Genre: Animal Romantic Comedy  |  Vertical 9:16 Format

Production Notes

Genre: AI-generated animal romantic comedy / enemies-to-lovers

Target Audience: 18–40 viewers; dog content fans; romantic comedy crossover audience; legal drama fans

Format: Vertical 9:16 short-form; close-up on both animals and human faces; each episode 60–90 seconds

Animal V.O. Protocol: BISCUIT (Isabella's golden retriever, male, scrappy/chaotic energy) and BUTTER (Damian's golden retriever, female, elegant/knowing energy) have full internal V.O. narration — italicized, labeled BISCUIT (V.O.) and BUTTER (V.O.). Neither speaks to humans. All animal-to-human communication is through body language. Animals communicate with each other through action — close-up on their faces for emotional reaction shots.

Key Characters: ISABELLA (30s, sharp attorney, beautiful, has been in litigation mode so long she's forgotten how to be a person), DAMIAN (30s, controlled businessman, handsome, the opposing party — their companies have been suing each other for 3 years), BISCUIT (Isabella's golden retriever, male, agent of chaos and romance), BUTTER (Damian's golden retriever, female, elegant, clearly the smarter dog)

Season Arc: Two dogs understand before their owners do that the lawsuit is keeping two people who should be together apart. The dogs solve the problem by refusing to observe boundaries.

Episode 1
The Lawsuit
Runtime: ~70s

INT. COURTROOM / EXT. CITY PARK — DAY (SPLIT SCREEN INTERCUT)

A courtroom. ISABELLA stands at the plaintiff's table — composed, exact, reading from a motion. Across the aisle: DAMIAN. He doesn't look at her. She doesn't look at him. Their lawyers talk at each other like weapons.

Isabella
(to the judge, clipped)
"This is the forty-seventh motion in eighteen months, Your Honor. We are seeking summary judgment."
Damian's Lawyer
"Denied. We have new discovery."

INTERCUT: Across town. City park. BISCUIT — golden retriever, big ears, energy of a puppy who never grew up — is running in wide circles on a lawn. BUTTER — golden retriever, precise, graceful — trots carefully on a path. Their DOG WALKERS are on their phones, not watching.

Biscuit (V.O.)
"There is a smell. The best smell. No — the BEST smell I have EVER—"

Biscuit spots Butter. Full stop. His entire body goes still for one full second. Then he bolts toward her.

Butter (V.O.)
"Oh no."
⚡ Cliffhanger

Biscuit arrives at full speed and skids to a stop six inches from Butter's nose — close-up on both their faces, nose to nose — and Butter blinks once, slowly, like a queen deciding.

Episode 2
Biscuit & Butter
Runtime: ~75s

EXT. CITY PARK — CONTINUOUS

The leashes have tangled. Biscuit and Butter have circled each other twice in opposite directions and are now lashed together by a double knot of nylon. Their walkers are panicking. Isabella and Damian arrive simultaneously — called from different directions.

Biscuit (V.O.)
"This was not intentional. I want that on the record. But I am not upset about it."
Butter (V.O.)
"It was completely intentional on his part. I am choosing to allow it."

Isabella and Damian reach the dogs at the same moment. They are three inches apart. Neither backs up. They look down at the knotted leashes, then up at each other.

Isabella
(controlled)
"Your dog is sitting on my shoe."
Damian
"Your dog ate my leash."
Isabella
"It's nylon. He's fine."
Damian
"It was Italian leather."

They both crouch at the same time to untangle the leashes. Their hands collide. Both pull back simultaneously.

⚡ Cliffhanger

Biscuit sits squarely between them and lays one large paw on each of their hands — looking from one face to the other with absolute satisfaction.

Episode 3
Separation Attempt
Runtime: ~75s

INT. ISABELLA'S APARTMENT / EXT. CITY STREETS — DAY

Isabella on the phone, pacing. Biscuit watches her from his bed, head tilted.

Isabella
(to her assistant)
"I need a dog walker, full-time, six days a week, someone who does not under any circumstances take him to Riverside Park."
Biscuit (V.O.)
"Oh. Oh she thinks this will work."

INTERCUT: Damian's office. Same call, essentially. Same decision.

Damian
(into phone)
"Professional walker. New route. No parks we've used before."
Butter (V.O.)
"Bless his heart."

Day three of new arrangement. New walkers, new routes. Biscuit and Butter are being walked on opposite sides of the city. Then — simultaneously, somehow — they both bolt. Biscuit pulls his leash free; Butter unclips herself. Twenty minutes later, Isabella and Damian receive the same call: "The dogs are at Café Soleil on 5th. Together. The door was propped open. They just — walked in."

Biscuit (V.O.)
"I looked up the address last time. Memorized it. Butter agreed on a time."
⚡ Cliffhanger

Isabella walks into Café Soleil and finds Biscuit and Butter curled together under a corner table — and Damian is already standing across the café, arms crossed, waiting, looking at her the way you look at someone you're not supposed to find funny.

Episode 4
The Café
Runtime: ~80s

INT. CAFÉ SOLEIL — DAY

Isabella and Damian sit across from each other. Two coffees — ordered separately. The dogs are asleep under the table, curled together like a braid of gold fur. A café worker has draped a small blanket over them.

Butter (V.O.)
"They keep starting sentences and not finishing them. It's adorable and excruciating."
Damian
"I've reviewed the deposition schedule."
Isabella
"Don't talk to me about the deposition schedule here."
Damian
"Then what do we talk about?"
Isabella
(a beat)
"Your dog. She's clearly the smart one."
Damian
"Don't tell Biscuit that."
Isabella
"He knows."

They both glance down. The dogs are sleeping peacefully. Somehow 45 minutes have passed. A waiter refills their coffees without being asked. Both of them realize it at the same moment.

Damian
"We've been here forty-five minutes."
Isabella
"I know."
⚡ Cliffhanger

Neither of them reaches for the check — and neither of them stands up.

Episode 5
Their Lawyers Find Out
Runtime: ~75s

INT. LAW OFFICES / ISABELLA'S APARTMENT — DAY

Isabella's lawyer calls. She puts it on speaker. Her face throughout this call is the study in controlled guilt.

Lawyer (V.O.)
(alarmed)
"Isabella, I have it on very good authority that you sat in a café with Damian Reyes for forty-seven minutes last Thursday."
Isabella
"The dogs ran away. Together. We retrieved them."
Lawyer (V.O.)
"I'm issuing a no-contact order."
Isabella
"You can't issue a no-contact order over a dog situation."
Lawyer (V.O.)
"I absolutely can and I absolutely am."

Biscuit is watching this from across the room. Close-up on his face.

Biscuit (V.O.)
"No-contact order. I've been through worse. I have a plan."

That evening. Biscuit is in Isabella's apartment. Then he isn't. She calls. She texts the walker. She checks the building. Then — her phone rings. Damian's number. She stares at it.

⚡ Cliffhanger

Isabella picks up the call and says nothing — just waits — and Damian's voice comes through: "Your dog is at my building. Security has him. He's asking for something. I think it's me."

Episode 6
How Did He Get Here
Runtime: ~70s

INT. DAMIAN'S BUILDING LOBBY — NIGHT

Damian is in the lobby with Biscuit. The dog is perfectly calm — tail wagging, sitting nicely, thoroughly pleased with himself. The SECURITY GUARD shrugs. He was just here.

Biscuit (V.O.)
"Six blocks. Subway entrance to subway exit. I followed the smell of Butter's walker's jacket. I am a genius."
Damian
(to Biscuit, sitting on the lobby bench with him)
"This is a violation of two separate legal documents. You know that."

Biscuit puts his head in Damian's lap. Damian looks down at him. He does not remove the dog's head. He picks up his phone.

He types to Isabella. The text exchange is shown close on the screen:

Damian (text)
"Your dog is here. He seems happy. He ate half my dinner."
Isabella (text)
"I'll come get him."
Damian (text)
"It's late. He can stay. I'll walk him in the morning."

Pause. Three dots appear on Isabella's side. Then disappear. Then appear again.

⚡ Cliffhanger

Close on Damian's phone screen as Isabella's reply comes through: "Okay." — just that one word — and Damian reads it twice, and almost smiles.

Episode 7
The Texts
Runtime: ~80s

INT. ISABELLA'S APARTMENT / DAMIAN'S APARTMENT — NIGHT (SPLIT SCREEN)

The text thread. We see it building across the screen — both phones, both faces illuminated by screen glow.

Damian (text)
"He found Butter's bed. He's in it. She's next to it."
Isabella (text)
"She must be furious."
Damian (text)
"She's watching him like a documentary."

A pause in the thread. Then, at 1:04 AM:

Damian (text)
"Biscuit is asleep on my couch."
Isabella (text)
"That would be fine."
Damian (text)
"He snores."
Isabella (text)
"I know."

Both of them in their separate apartments. Both of them looking at the phone and not at anything else.

Butter (V.O.)
"They're texting. About us. It's about them. They don't know yet."
Biscuit (V.O.)
"She said 'I know.' That's intimate. Humans use 'I know' when they mean something else."
⚡ Cliffhanger

The thread sits open on both their phones with that last message — "I know" — and neither of them types anything, and neither of them puts the phone down.

Episode 8
Morning
Runtime: ~80s

INT. ISABELLA'S APARTMENT — MORNING

A knock. Isabella, still in pajamas — blue plaid — opens the door with coffee in hand, fully expecting the grocery delivery. It is Damian, in a coat, Biscuit on a leash at his side. Neither of them is ready for this moment.

Damian
(taking in the pajamas)
"I said I'd walk him in the morning."
Isabella
"It's seven AM."
Damian
"He woke me at six-fifteen."

Biscuit walks calmly into the apartment. Damian stays in the doorway. They hand off the leash — his fingers, her fingers, the leather strap between them. They both hold on for a half-second too long.

Damian
(not quite looking at her)
"The dogs think this lawsuit is stupid."
Isabella
(a beat)
"The dogs are not parties to it."
Damian
"No. They're just living through it."

She closes the door. Biscuit sits next to her and looks up at her face.

Biscuit (V.O.)
"You're welcome."
⚡ Cliffhanger

Isabella leans her back against the closed door and stands there for a moment, holding the leash with both hands, looking at the door — and then very slowly smiles.

Episode 9
The Deposition
Runtime: ~80s

INT. LAW OFFICES — DEPOSITION ROOM — DAY

A formal deposition room. Court reporter. Two lawyers on each side. Isabella and Damian across the table from each other — suits on, faces neutral. This is their first official face-to-face in months. Biscuit is present as Isabella's "emotional support animal," wearing a vest he clearly finds undignified.

Biscuit (V.O.)
"The vest says SUPPORT. I prefer AGENT OF CHANGE. But fine."
Damian's Lawyer
"Ms. Reyes-Okonkwo, please state your position on the December amendment—"

Biscuit, hitherto perfectly still, suddenly stands. Walks directly across the length of the deposition table — across papers, across the recorder, across everyone's dignity — and sits down directly in front of Damian. Looks at him. Tail wagging.

Isabella
(mortified)
"Biscuit. Down."

Biscuit does not move. He is looking at Damian like he is explaining something.

Damian
(very calm)
"He's fine."

Damian looks at Biscuit. Biscuit looks at Damian. Damian reaches out and scratches behind Biscuit's ears. The lawyers all look at each other.

⚡ Cliffhanger

Damian looks up from Biscuit to Isabella, and they are suddenly looking directly at each other for the first time in three years without a motive between them — and the court reporter stops typing.

Episode 10
Settlement Talks 🔒 Paywall
Runtime: ~90s

INT. NEUTRAL CONFERENCE ROOM — DAY

Both sets of lawyers present their joint recommendation: settlement. Both clients present. The lawyers look surprised by their own agreement. Isabella and Damian look at their respective lawyers and say the same word at the same moment.

Isabella + Damian
(simultaneously)
"Yes."

The lawyers file out to draft terms. Isabella and Damian are alone in the room. Both dogs were allegedly left at home today. A beat of quiet.

Isabella
"I was going to bring Biscuit."
Damian
(a pause)
"I know."
Isabella
"I decided it was better to— why are you reaching under the table?"

Damian reaches under the conference table. A leash. He lifts — and BUTTER walks out from under the table where she has been sitting quietly the entire meeting. She sits between them both, tail sweeping the floor.

Butter (V.O.)
"You're welcome. Now settle the lawsuit, you absolute disasters."

Isabella stares at Butter. Then at Damian. A beat. Then another.

⚡ Cliffhanger

Isabella starts laughing — really laughing, for the first time in three years in a room with him — and Damian watches her like he has been waiting for exactly this and did not know it until now.

Episode 11
The Settlement
Runtime: ~80s

INT. LAW OFFICES — SIGNING ROOM — DAY

Two pens. Two copies. Four people. Biscuit and Butter are both present and seated side by side, watching with the gravity of attorneys.

Biscuit (V.O.)
"Three years, two months, eleven days. For them. For us it was about six weeks."
Butter (V.O.)
"For me it was the first day. He ran at me in a park at maximum speed. I knew then."

The documents are signed. Lawyers shake hands and leave quickly, relieved. Isabella and Damian are alone again. They stand by the table, settlement signed.

Damian
"Forty minutes."
Isabella
"What?"
Damian
"It took us forty minutes to settle. Three years of billing, forty minutes."
Isabella
"I can't explain that."

They walk out together. Both leashes. The leashes tangle on the doorframe as they pass through simultaneously. Both dogs look up at them.

⚡ Cliffhanger

Isabella and Damian both reach down to untangle the leashes — and this time, neither of them pulls their hands back.

Episode 12
Same Park
Runtime: ~85s

EXT. RIVERSIDE PARK — MORNING

The same park from episode one. Morning light. Same bench. Isabella arrives with two coffees. Damian is already there. She hands him one without asking how he takes it. He takes it. He already knows she knows.

Biscuit (V.O.)
"We are back at the beginning. But everything is different."
Butter (V.O.)
"Everything is exactly as it was supposed to be."

Biscuit and Butter are off-leash on the lawn in front of the bench, running together in wide arcs. Close on their faces — unbridled joy.

Isabella
(looking straight ahead)
"This doesn't mean anything."
Damian
(also not looking at her)
"Of course not."
Isabella
"We're just two people. With dogs. On a bench."
Damian
"Who happened to have the same time free."
Isabella
"Exactly."

A pause. Biscuit and Butter barrel back and curl together at their feet. Damian's arm is on the back of the bench. Isabella's shoulder is almost under it.

Biscuit (V.O.)
"They're still lying. But they're lying together. That's progress."
⚡ Cliffhanger

Isabella's head tilts very slightly toward Damian's shoulder — not touching, not yet — and Damian looks down at her profile and says nothing, just brings the coffee to his mouth to hide whatever his face is doing.
